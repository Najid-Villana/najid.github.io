The Problem with Traditional Fire Safety

Smoke detectors wait for smoke to reach the ceiling. By then, it's often too late.

Slow Response Time

Traditional smoke detectors can take 2-5 minutes to trigger, allowing fires to spread significantly before detection.

High False Alarm Rate

Industrial environments trigger false alarms 30% of the time, leading to costly shutdowns and reduced trust in the system.

No Automated Response

Traditional systems only sound alarms. There's no automated suppression until firefighters arrive, losing critical response time.
